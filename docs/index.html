The Unit Conversion tool allows you to convert measurements between a wide range of units. These include various different types of units, such as those of mass, length, and time, and different systems of units within each general type, such as metric units and US customary units.

To get started, choose a unit type from the "Unit Type: " dropdown menu, and then choose the two desired units from the two dropdowns. Then, enter a measurement in either text box, and the equivalent quantity in terms of the other unit will be displayed in the other text box. You can also adjust certain settings in the settings dropdown, which has a gear icon on its dropdown toggler.

To view this documentation from the Unit Converter tool, click the question mark icon in the navbar at the top of the screen, or click the Documentation button in the hamburger menu.
